That is why I was happy to discover Code your Future course as it might be a great opportunity to change my career path. I am really excited about learning the new skills thanks to this course and there are a lot of reasons why I would like to continue to be a part of Code Your Future community. The main reason why I like tech industry is an opportunity to constantly learn and grow. I have always been enjoying learning new things and achieving new goals. It is a sector filled with creativity and I would be happy to improve my problem solving skills and learn how to invent new solutions. What is also important that tech industry continues to thrive and there is constant increase in demand for people with technical skills.
